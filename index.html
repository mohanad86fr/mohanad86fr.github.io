Travel to Paris

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

Top Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower : Gustave Eiffel's iconic, wrought-iron 1889 tower, with steps and elevators to observation decks
  2. Louvre Museum : Former historic palace housing huge art collection, from Roman sculptures to da Vinci's "Mona Lisa."
  3. Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Paris : Towering, 13th-century cathedral with flying buttresses & gargoyles, setting for Hugo's novel.
  4. Arc de Triomphe : Iconic triumphal arch built to commemorate Napoleon's victories, with an observation deck.
